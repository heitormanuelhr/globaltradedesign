About Us

Global Trade Design was built on more than 20 years of hands-on experience in international trade, supply chain design, and cross-border market development.

We operate as trade architects, not intermediaries. Our role is to design, structure and execute international trade projects that are sustainable, compliant, and scalable.

With deep expertise in international regulations, logistics, procurement and market entry, we support companies across multiple industries by connecting buyers and sellers and managing the full commercial and operational process, from strategy to final delivery.

Industries We Serve

    Our regulatory expertise and operational capabilities allow us to operate across complex and highly regulated industries:

  • Food & Beverage

    • FMCG, RTE products, fresh fruit, raw materials, labeling and food safety compliance.

  • Petrochemical & Industrial Materials

    • Hazardous materials, oversized equipment, high-risk transport and compliance.

  • Aerospace & Automotive

    • Specialized machinery, components, oversized cargo and secure logistics execution.

    From fresh produce containers to oversized industrial equipment, we design the right commercial and logistical structure for every operation.
